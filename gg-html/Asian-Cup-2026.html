Tournament format
Group Stage: two groups, each team plays against all other teams in their group.
The best 2 teams from each group advance to the Playoffs:

  • Semi-finals: 1st team from one group vs 2nd team from another group
  • Match for the bronze medals
  • Grand Final

All matches are 5 vs 5 duels in a best-of-3 format.
